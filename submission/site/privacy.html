Dendrite — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-21

English · 简体中文

Dendrite ("the app") is an AI-native, non-linear knowledge space. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data. We operate no servers and run no backend of our own.

What we collect

Nothing. We have no servers, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no account system. We never receive, see, or store your conversations, files, or API keys.

Where your data lives

  • Conversations, branches, bookmarks, and attachments are stored only in a database on your own device.
  • API keys you enter are stored in the operating system's secure credential store (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) on your device — never in plaintext and never transmitted to us.

Data sent to third-party AI providers

To answer you, the app sends the relevant part of your conversation to the AI provider you configure (for example NVIDIA NIM, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini), over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection, using your own API key. You may instead choose an on-device model, in which case nothing leaves your device. Your use of a third-party provider is governed by that provider's own privacy policy and terms.

Permissions

The app requests internet access solely to reach the AI provider you choose. No location, contacts, camera, or microphone access is required for core use.

Data deletion

Because all data is on your device, you control it entirely: delete individual conversations in the app, or uninstall the app to remove everything.

Children

Dendrite is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Dendrite — 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-06-21

Dendrite（「本应用」）是一个 AI 原生、非线性的知识空间。本政策说明本应用如何处理 你的数据。我们不运营任何服务器，也没有自己的后端。

我们采集什么

什么都不采集。我们没有服务器、没有统计分析、没有广告 SDK、也没有账号系统。 我们永远不会接收、看到或存储你的对话、文件或 API 密钥。

你的数据存在哪里

  • 对话、分支、书签与附件仅存储在你自己设备上的数据库中。
  • 你填入的 API 密钥存放在操作系统的安全凭据存储（Android Keystore / iOS Keychain）里——绝不明文保存，也绝不会传给我们。

发送给第三方 AI 服务商的数据

为了给出回答，本应用会通过加密（HTTPS）连接、使用你自己的 API 密钥，把对话中相关 的部分发送给你所配置的 AI 服务商（例如 NVIDIA NIM、OpenAI、Anthropic 或 Google Gemini）。你也可以选择端侧本地模型，此时不会有任何数据离开你的设备。 你对第三方服务商的使用，受该服务商自身的隐私政策与条款约束。

权限

本应用仅为访问你所选择的 AI 服务商而申请网络权限。核心功能不需要定位、通讯录、相机 或麦克风权限。

数据删除

由于所有数据都在你的设备上，你拥有完全的控制权：在应用内删除单条对话，或卸载应用即可 清除全部数据。

儿童

Dendrite 并非面向 13 岁以下儿童。

政策变更

若本政策有变更，上方「最后更新」日期会相应更新。
